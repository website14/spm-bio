UM BIOINFORMATICS SOCIAL ENGAGEMENT PROJECT

An initiative by first year Bioinformatics students of University of Malaya to help a vulnerable group of society.(SPM students 2020).
A survery was carried out to identify the topics of Form 4 and Form 5 that students struggle with and the students were aided with access to online notes for the said topics.
